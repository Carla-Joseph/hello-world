Most high schools have a freshman, junior varsity and varsity team. The most talented players will form the varsity team and will have the opportunity to showcase their talents to win a scholarship and compete at the next level.

College Basketball

College basketball is the national stage for student-athletes who want to join a professional team. Every college team is part of a conference. An athlete's exposure to the professional community is greater when they join a good conference which allows them to compete against the top tier athletes.

Professional Basketball

An Athlete who wants to play professional basketball will attempt to join a team in the National Basketball Association or a team in a foreign market.
